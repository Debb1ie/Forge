Introduction

FORGE is an AI-powered website creation platform. Describe your vision in plain language and FORGE translates it into production-ready HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — or React components — instantly.

Unlike traditional website builders, FORGE doesn't ask you to drag and drop. It asks you to think. Your words become layouts. Your tone becomes typography. Your mood becomes color.

forge.build("A luxury perfume brand with editorial photography, dark backgrounds, and parallax scroll")

The result is clean, semantic, export-ready code you can host anywhere — or deploy directly through FORGE hosting in one click.

Core Concepts

Every site built on FORGE starts with a Prompt — a plain-language description of what you want. The more specific your prompt, the more tailored the result.

You can further shape the output by selecting a Visual Style: Dark Luxury, Minimal Clean, Brutalist, Neo-Retro, Organic, or Data-Viz. Each style applies a cohesive aesthetic system including fonts, spacing, color palette, and animation defaults.
